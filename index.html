About Me


Hi, my name is Sveto Stojcevic. In the past 20 years I have been working as a IT Consultant mainly as a VBA Programmer.

My last contract in Australia was with Commonwealth Bank and lasted for seven years.

In 2007 I went to visit my old parents in Macedonia (part of former Yugoslavia). I thought I will stay couple of months. I stayed 10 years. After being about a year in Macedonia I got permanent job with the Macedonian Govenment. In October 2017 I resigned to return in Australia.

My goal is to be a full stack developer so that I have all the skills needed to build a website. My expert skills in VBA can be handy as managment accross the industry is using Excel extenisvely.
